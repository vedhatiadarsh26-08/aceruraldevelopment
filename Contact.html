ACE Rural Developers Pvt. Ltd. is a visionary enterprise committed to redefining rural development in India, with a special focus on Telangana’s heartland. Rooted in sustainability, innovation, and cultural heritage, we aim to transform scattered, underutilized rural land into vibrant ecosystems that foster economic empowerment and community wellness.


With over 70 to 100 acres under our fold, we focus on high-impact initiatives including:


  • Agro-based clusters and organic farming zones

  • Export-oriented processing hubs for agriculture and wellness products

  • Eco-tourism and wellness retreats rooted in rural traditions

  • Skill development, renewable energy, and rural employment zones


At ACE, we believe rural is the future. We envision strategic collaborations with like-minded institutions, investors, and infrastructure leaders to scale our impact — with a clear mission to create self-sustaining rural economies, boost exports from the grassroots, and foster environmentally conscious development.
